Our Story

Made by Hand, Inspired by the Sea

Songbird Boutique started with a love for sewing and a dream of creating beautiful, handmade pieces that carry the warmth of the coast. Every stitch tells a story — from the fabrics we choose to the designs we embroider.

We believe in slow fashion, quality craftsmanship, and the magic of something made just for you.
