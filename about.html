The Beginning

From a Love of Sewing to a Small Business Dream

Songbird Boutique was born from a simple love of creating beautiful things by hand. What started as a hobby — sewing bags for friends, embroidering designs on linen scraps — grew into something much more.

Inspired by the relaxed beauty of coastal living and the free-spirited charm of bohemian style, every piece we create blends these two worlds. We believe fashion should be thoughtful, personal, and made to last.

Each item in our shop is cut, sewn, and embroidered by hand in our home studio. No mass production, no shortcuts — just honest craftsmanship and a whole lot of love.
